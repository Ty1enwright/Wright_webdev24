Board And Training Packages

Full Service Board
Full care board includes a 12x12 stall, daily claning, twice daily feeding of grain and supplements and hay, daily turn out (as weather permits). All stall bedding, hay, and grain is included in your board.
Partial Board
Partial Board includes a 12x12 stall, twice daily feeding of grain and supplements and hay, and daliy turn out (as weather permits). All stall bedding, hay, and grain is included in your board. With partial board you are responsible for cleaning your own Stalls at least once a day. Partial boards who fail to keep their stalls clean will haves their stall cleaned by our staff and will incur Aadditional changes.
Pasture Board
Horses on pasture board have free roam to 20 acers of pasture and have access to several run-in shelters. Grain, hay,and water is provided as part of your pasture board. Blanketing is available Charge, as needed.
